UUIDs.net

Secure file storage and sharing

Ed Snowden revealed in 2013 that almost all data stored on the internet is not private. We created UUIDs.net to store and share files privately, securely and anonymously.

How are we different?

We never ask for your name or email address. Our service doesn't record a log of access times, addresses or locations. We are hosted in Iceland because the International Modern Media Institute protects anonymous sources and internet service providers. We're also green and our software is free and open source.

What does it cost?

All new accounts receive 2GB of free quota for data transfer plus data storage. Extra quotas of 20GB cost 0.05 Bitcoin each (about $30 USD). Each year your quota is reduced by the average amount of data stored. For example you could upload a 5GB file, securely store it for a whole year and share it twice for 0.05 Bitcoin.
