Our Terms & Conditions

In the following, "service" refers to the secure internet storage service at UUIDs.net provided by Kevin Hutchinson and associates (henceforth "we").

Our Commitment to You

We cannot guarantee that the service will always be available, but we will do our best to make it available at all times. We promise that all uploaded files are encrypted before storage, and that our encryption renders files unreadable to anyone except the uploader (and those with whom the file has been shared by the uploader), the only exception being if the uploader allows a third party to know the account name and password, or any of the links to shared files.

Your Commitment to Us

By signing up to use the service, you accept and agree to the following:

  1. You may not store any data that is considered illegal in Iceland or the EU.
  2. You may not use the service for activities considered illegal in Iceland or the EU.
  3. You accept that we may revoke access to your account at any time without reason.
  4. You accept that, although very unlikely, your stored data may be lost.
  5. You promise to say good things about us to your friends and family!

Requesting a Shared Link be Disabled

If a publicly shared link to a stored file is deemed to break Iceland or EU laws, please email full details to uuids.net@gmail.com and we will:

  1. Remove the file content relating to the shared link
  2. Disable the link so that the file may no longer be accessed
  3. Disable the account that uploaded the file to prevent further abuse
